About

myself

My love for coding developed when I started playing computer games and interacting with some fun applications, I really wanted to know how to create my own application with all the ideas I had in my mind, so I used to watch youtube tutorials and do research on coding. Even though my friends told me coding is hard, I really liked the challenge so I decided to join Moringa school. In Moringa school I spent over 1200 hours writing code, the learning system there was a new thing for me since its student based learning and I learned a lot on my own. I gained skills in HTML, CSS, javascript, java and I specialized in android development. I also learned how to work in teams and collaboration using git and also improved my soft skills in presentation as I got experience from the several project presentations and idea pitching I had. I worked as a scrum master in a group of four for an application that was meant to make customers experience when ordering food smoother and faster.
